About Us

Kaer Morhen is the ancient stronghold of the Witchers. For centuries, it has served as a place of learning, training, and refuge for monster slayers and travelers alike.

Our Training

We offer combat training, alchemy workshops, monster lore education, and survival techniques for those brave enough to walk the Path.
